History

Mwatulu's International Hotel tooks inspiration from Kentucky Fried Chicken alias KFC. The idea was to create something good using locally available food stuff to create not only good and pocket friendly dishes but dishes that would provide the body with energy to do daly tasks and give a sense of satisfaction to the consumer.

Our founder, Mr Mike Horton, believed in the idea of food is the solution to everything. For example if one wanted to feel energized, they could eat ugali and beef and if another wanted something expsensive, something exotic then they would pick pilau or another tasty dish to meet their needs.

location

Mwatulu's International Hotel was Located near kisi university when it started in 2013 before moving to kisii town near the Central Business District in 2016

Since then we have been providing residents wiht sweet and nutritious dishes that are affordable and healthy to the body.As of 2022, we have grown and now we have branches in almost 40 counties in Kenya and we have branches in over 10 African countries Mainly in Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, Burundi, Somalia, Ethiopia etc.
